About me

Hi! Im a web developer that just came out from the oven (I just finished my formation). I come from a scientific background, I studied Biochemistry and I specialises in neuroscience research. But my journey in tech began after I moved to south of France and I wanted a job that I can do from anywhere (or at least not having to look for a lab in my area of expertise). Since then, I’ve had the opportunity to learn by myself watching videos and playing games (codecombat is really nice to start coding).

I’m passionate about crafting beautiful, functional websites and web applications, and I love tackling new challenges and opportunities that push me to grow and deliver exceptional results. The (tech) world evolves rapidly, and I’m committed to continuous learning, and I strive to bring creativity, innovation, and strategy to every project.

Outside of work, you can find me with my cats and my family, enjoying the beach and probable planning a new trip for me or some of my friends. I’m always eager to connect with like-minded professionals or potential collaborators, so feel free to reach out if you’d like to work together or learn more about what I can offer.
